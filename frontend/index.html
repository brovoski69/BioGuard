ABOUT BIOGUARD

BioGuard is a virtual biomechanics laboratory that simulates joint stress across everyday activities. By combining your unique body profile with physics-based force models, we calculate real-time risk scores for each major joint — helping you make informed decisions about your physical activities and long-term joint health.
